Flags - hold one bit each (true or false)

--------------------------------
|  Flag  |   Description    |
-----------------------------
|   CF   |      Carry       |
-----------------------------
|   PF   |      Parity      |
-----------------------------
|   ZF   |      Zero        |
-----------------------------
|   SF   |      Sign        |
-----------------------------
|   OF   |    Overflow      |
-----------------------------
|   AF   |      Adjust      |
-----------------------------
|   IF   | Interrupt Enabled|
-----------------------------
